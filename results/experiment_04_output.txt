MODEL RESPONSE:
### Game Name: **Gold Rush Gulch**

**Theme:** Two rival prospectors are racing to scavenge gold nuggets from a dangerous, shifting canyon floor before a storm hits.

---

### 1. Objective
Be the first player to collect **5 Gold Nuggets** or have the most nuggets when the board is cleared.

---

### 2. Setup
*   **The Board:** A 5x5 grid of squares.
*   **The Treasure:** Place 10 Gold Nuggets (tokens) randomly on the squares. No square can hold more than one nugget.
*   **The Players:** Each player places their "Prospector" pawn on opposite corners of the board.
*   **The Obstacles:** Place 3 "Rock" tokens on any empty squares to block movement.

---

### 3. Turn Structure
Players take turns performing **two actions**. You may choose the same action twice or two different actions:
1.  **Move:** Move your pawn one square (orthogonally—up, down, left, or right). You cannot move through Rocks or the other player.
2.  **Scavenge:** If you are on a square with a Gold Nugget, pick it up and add it to your inventory.
3.  **Shift:** Choose one "Rock" token and move it to any adjacent empty square.

---

### 4. Rules
*   **Collision:** You cannot move into a square occupied by the other player.
*   **Inventory:** Players keep their collected nuggets in a visible pile beside the board. 
*   **The Shifting Canyon:** If you end your turn on a square that was adjacent to a Rock, you may move that Rock one space as a *free action* (this does not count toward your two main actions).
*   **Exhaustion:** If a player is completely blocked by Rocks and the other player, they must skip their turn, and the opponent may choose to move one Rock anywhere on the board to "clear a path."

---

### 5. Winning Condition
*   **Instant Win:** The game ends immediately when a player collects their **5th Gold Nugget**.
*   **Board Clear:** If all 10 Gold Nuggets have been collected and nobody has 5, the player with the most nuggets wins. 
*   **Tie-Breaker:** If both players have 5 nuggets, the player who collected their 5th nugget first wins.

---

### Strategy Tips:
*   **Blocking:** Use your "Shift" actions to wall off your opponent from clusters of gold.
*   **Efficiency:** Plan your movement so you end your turn next to a Rock, giving you the free "Shift" action to sabotage your opponent’s path on your next turn.
*   **Aggression:** Sometimes it is better to move a Rock to block a path than to move your own character.
