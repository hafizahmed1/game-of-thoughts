Based on the text provided, here are the rules for Tic Tac Toe:

*   **Setup:** Draw a 3x3 grid (two horizontal parallel lines intersected by two vertical parallel lines) on a piece of paper using a writing implement (pencil, pen, or crayon).
*   **Players:** The game requires exactly two players: Player One and Player Two. Any additional people are considered observers.
*   **Starting:** Determine who is Player One and who is Player Two by any method (e.g., Rock, Paper, Scissors).
*   **Turns:** Players take turns placing their designated symbol into an unoccupied space on the grid.
    *   Player One uses Xs.
    *   Player Two uses Os.
*   **Objective:** The goal is to be the first to get three of your symbols in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally).
*   **Winning:** The first player to achieve three in a row wins the game.
*   **Drawing:** If the grid is completely filled with Xs and Os without either player achieving three in a row, the game ends in a draw.
*   **Constraint:** You can only place a symbol in an unoccupied space within the 3x3 grid. The space outside the grid is not valid for placement.
