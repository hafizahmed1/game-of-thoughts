Here are the rules for Tic-Tac-Toe:

*   **Grid:** The game is played on a 3x3 grid.
*   **Players:** There are two players, one using "X" and one using "O."
*   **Turns:** Players take turns placing their mark on the board.
*   **Placement:** A player can place their mark in any empty square on the grid.
*   **Winning Condition:** The first player to successfully place three of their marks in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) wins the game.
*   **End of Game:** The game ends once a player wins or the board is completely filled.
