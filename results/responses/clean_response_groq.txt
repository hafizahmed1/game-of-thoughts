Here are the rules of the board game in bullet points:

* The game is played on a 3x3 grid.
* Player One places an X in an empty space on the grid.
* Player Two places an O in an empty space on the grid.
* Players alternate turns, with Player One placing an X and Player Two placing an O.
* The game continues until one of the following conditions is met:
  * A player has three of their symbols (X or O) in a row, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.
  * The grid is completely filled with no player having three in a row.
* If a player has three of their symbols in a row, they win the game.
* If the grid is completely filled and no player has three in a row, the game is a draw.
