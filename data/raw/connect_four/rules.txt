Connect Four is played on a vertical grid with 7 columns and 6 rows.

Two players take turns dropping their pieces into one of the columns.

Before the game begins, the board is empty and the starting player is chosen at random.

On each turn, a player drops one piece into any column that is not already full.

The piece falls to the lowest available space in that column.

Once placed, a piece does not move again, and pieces are not captured or removed.

A player wins by forming a line of four of their own pieces horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

If the board becomes completely full and neither player has formed a line of four, the game ends in a draw.
